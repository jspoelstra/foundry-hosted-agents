# Seattle Activity Options

## Downtown

- Pike Place Market food walk (iconic, moderate cost, mostly outdoor)
- Seattle Art Museum (indoor fallback)
- Seattle Aquarium + waterfront stroll (family-friendly)
- Ferry ride from Colman Dock (iconic, budget-flexible)

## Capitol Hill

- Volunteer Park Conservatory (low-cost, indoor/outdoor mix)
- Elliott Bay Book Company + cafe loop (low-cost, indoor fallback)
- Live music venues on Pike/Pine (nightlife)

## Fremont

- Fremont Sunday Market (low-cost, local makers)
- Burke-Gilman / canal trail walk (outdoors)
- Brewery tasting flight (adult group activity)

## Weather fallback ideas

- Chihuly Garden and Glass (mostly indoor with covered transitions)
- MoPOP exhibits (indoor)
- Seattle Central Library architecture walk (indoor, low-cost)
